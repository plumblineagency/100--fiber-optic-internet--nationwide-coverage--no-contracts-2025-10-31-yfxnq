About FiberNet

Leading the fiber optic revolution across the nation

Our Story

Founded in 2015, FiberNet was born from a simple vision: to bring world-class fiber optic internet to every corner of America. What started as a small regional provider has grown into one of the nation's most trusted fiber internet companies, serving hundreds of thousands of customers across all 50 states. Our founders recognized that internet connectivity had become essential to modern life, yet many Americans were stuck with outdated cable and DSL technology. We invested heavily in building a next-generation fiber optic infrastructure, partnering with local communities and working tirelessly to expand our network where it was needed most. Today, our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction remains unwavering.

Our Mission

At FiberNet, our mission is simple yet powerful: to democratize access to ultra-fast, reliable internet for everyone. We believe that everyone deserves gigabit-speed connectivity without breaking the bank or being locked into restrictive contracts. Our core values of transparency, reliability, and customer-first service guide every decision we make. We're committed to delivering the fastest speeds, the most affordable prices, and the best customer support in the industry. By eliminating contracts, locking in prices, and rewarding loyalty with exclusive offers like our Nintendo Switch promotion, we're redefining what it means to be an internet service provider. We envision a future where fiber optic internet is the standard everywhere, and we're determined to make that vision a reality, one community at a time.
